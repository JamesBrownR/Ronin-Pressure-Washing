What we do

Pressure Washing Services

Commercial-grade cleaning for every surface on your property. We use the right method for each job so you get a clean result without any damage.

Pressure Washing High-pressure water for hard surfaces like concrete, pavers, and brick. Lifts oil, dirt, and organic growth without chemicals.

Driveway & Paver Cleaning

Concrete driveways and paver surfaces take a beating in South Florida, between the heat, the rain, and the constant moisture, algae and mold build up fast. Tire marks, oil stains, and years of grime give your property a worn-down look even when everything else is well-kept.

We clean both concrete and paver surfaces using the right pressure for each, pulling out embedded dirt and restoring the original color without blasting the surface apart. If you have pavers with polymeric sand, we work around it.

  • Concrete driveways and walkways
  • Brick and paver surfaces
  • Pool decks and patios
  • Oil stain and tire mark removal
  • Algae and mold treatment

Soft Washing

Standard pressure washing tears up certain surfaces. Stucco, vinyl siding, screens, and painted wood can all be damaged if you hit them with the same pressure you'd use on a driveway. Soft washing solves that.

We apply a biodegradable cleaning solution at low pressure that kills the mold, mildew, algae, and bacteria at the root. The result lasts longer than pressure washing alone because the source of the discoloration is treated, not just rinsed away.

  • Stucco and painted exterior walls
  • Vinyl and aluminum siding
  • Screened enclosures
  • Outdoor furniture and surfaces
  • Fences and garden walls

Roof Cleaning

Those black streaks on your roof aren't just an eyesore -- they're Gloeocapsa magma, a cyanobacteria that feeds on the limestone in your shingles and tiles. Left alone, it shortens the life of your roof and can void your warranty. Most HOAs also require periodic roof cleaning.

We use soft wash for all roof cleaning. High-pressure water on a roof can crack tiles, blow off granules from shingles, and force water under the surface -- it's the wrong tool for the job. Our soft wash process removes algae, mold, and lichen safely and the results hold up far longer.

  • Tile and barrel tile roofs
  • Shingle roofs
  • Flat and low-slope roofs
  • Black streak and algae removal
  • Before-and-after photos included

Gutter & Fascia Cleaning

Gutters clogged with leaves, dirt, and organic debris back up water against your fascia and soffit. Over time that leads to rot, pest access, and water intrusion. The black streaks running down the outside of gutters -- called tiger stripes -- come from oxidized aluminum mixing with dirt and they don't rinse off with a hose.

We clear out gutter debris, treat tiger stripes on the exterior surface, and clean the fascia boards so the whole roofline looks uniform and functions the way it's supposed to.

  • Interior gutter flush and debris removal
  • Tiger stripe removal from gutter exteriors
  • Fascia and soffit cleaning
  • Downspout flushing

Fences & Decks

Wood fences and decks go gray and mossy fast in Florida's climate. Vinyl fences collect green and black algae that won't brush off. A good cleaning makes a bigger difference than most people expect before they see it done.

We match the method to the material. Vinyl and composite get soft washed. Wood gets a lower-pressure clean that removes the surface buildup without raising the grain or blowing out the wood fibers. If you're planning to stain or seal your deck after, a professional clean first is the right prep.

  • Wood fences and privacy panels
  • Vinyl and aluminum fencing
  • Wood and composite decks
  • Pergolas and outdoor structures
  • Pre-stain and pre-seal prep cleaning
